I along with 5 other teammates created a database management system for a hypothetical mall food court for a college SQL course.

## Mission Statement
Our goal is to develop a database management system that can help the manager track customers and orders within the entire food court. This will allow the restaurants to effectively staff employees around peak days and times as well as allow the mall to produce targeted marketing material.
